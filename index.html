My personal projects

The project which I'm most proud of is "My Training Schedules", a fitness app that I built with one of my friends.

It's been created with the purpose of allowing you to bring your schedules wherever you want in an easier and more flexible way than printing them on a paper sheet. We then added some interesting functions like the possibility to share your schedules with your friends and to search for new ones from the suggested and most popular ones. In the end we were also able to build a simple training tracking system which helps you during your workouts.

The app is now available on the Google Play store.
